ABOUT GRAY SCALE

Grayscale is a Bootstrap theme. It can be yours right now, simply download the templates on the preview page.the theme is open source, and you can use it for any purpose, personal or commercial.

This theme features stock photos by Gratisography along with a custom google maps skin courtesy of snazzy Maps. Grayscale includes full HTML,CSS and custom javascript files along with SASS and LESS files for easy customization
